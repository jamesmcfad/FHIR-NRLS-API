{{include.para}} {{include.name}}

See date for details on this parameter. The {{include.name}} parameter can be used as follows:

GET [baseUrl]/{{include.content}}?{{include.name}}=[date]{&{{include.name}}=[date]}

This SHOULD be supported for all consumer systems. When ‘{{include.name}}’ is included as a parameter it SHALL conform to the following:

- The {{include.name}} parameter SHALL only be included a maximum of two times in the request. - The first {{include.name}} parameter SHALL only be included once in the request. - The first {{include.name}} parameter SHALL be supplied with the ge search prefix. - The second {{include.name}} parameter SHALL only be included once in the request. - The second {{include.name}} parameter SHALL be supplied with the le search prefix.

The ‘{{include.name}}’ parameter is treated as a date range with a lower and an upper boundary.

The first {{include.name}} parameter SHALL be treated as the lower boundary.

GET [baseUrl]/{{include.content}}?{{include.name}}=ge2010-01-01&{{include.name}}=le2011-12-31

Return all {{include.content}} resources that have a {{include.name}} date greater than or equal to 1st Jan 2010, and a {{include.name}} date less than or equal to 31st Dec 2011.
